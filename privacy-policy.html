FAMILY CFO AI

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 21, 2026

Family CFO AI is designed as a privacy-first household finance app. This policy explains how the current mobile app handles information.

Information handled by the app

The app stores the financial information you enter, including accounts, balances, transactions, budgets, assets, and household settings. This information is stored in the app’s local database on your device. Financial-assistant answers are generated locally from that database and displayed in the current app session.

Family CFO AI does not require an account and does not operate an application server that receives this financial data.

Voice input

Voice input is optional and starts only after you press the microphone button and grant permission.

  • In device recognition mode, the app requests on-device speech recognition and does not intentionally upload recordings.
  • In system recognition mode, Apple, Google, or the device’s configured speech provider may process audio under that provider’s terms and privacy policy.
  • Family CFO AI uses the resulting transcript locally and does not retain an audio recording.

Exports and backups

CSV exports are not encrypted. They are created locally and leave the app only when you choose a destination through the system share sheet.

Full backups are encrypted with AES-GCM using a key protected by the device’s secure storage. You choose where to save or share the encrypted backup. A backup may become unreadable if its device-protected key is no longer available, including after clearing app data or uninstalling the app.

Analytics, advertising, and tracking

The current app does not include advertising, third-party analytics, cross-app tracking, or data-sale functionality. It does not use financial information to train a remote AI model.

Retention and deletion

Local data remains on the device until you delete supported records, clear the app’s data, or uninstall the app. Files exported outside the app remain under your control and must be deleted from their saved locations separately.

Permissions

The app requests microphone and speech-recognition permissions only for optional voice entry. File and sharing interfaces are used only when you choose to export or restore data.

Support communications

If you contact support by email or submit a public GitHub issue, the information you choose to provide is processed by the relevant email or GitHub service. Do not include account numbers, transaction details, backup files, or other sensitive financial information in a support request.

Children’s privacy

Family CFO AI is a general household-finance tool and is not directed to children. The app does not knowingly collect children’s personal information through an application server.

Changes and contact

Material changes to this policy will be reflected on this page and in its effective date. Questions, support requests, or privacy requests can be sent to xinheming22@gmail.com or submitted through the public support issue tracker.
